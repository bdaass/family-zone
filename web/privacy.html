Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Family Zone operates a mobile app and website for family fashion in Lebanon. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data we collect

  • Account data (email, name) when you register or sign in with Google
  • Favorites and cart stored in your account
  • Contact form messages (name, email, optional phone, message)
  • Product photos uploaded by staff for the catalogue
  • Basic technical data via Firebase for security and operation

How we use data

We use your data to run the store, process WhatsApp orders, respond to messages, and prevent abuse. We do not sell personal data.

Service providers

We use Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Hosting). See also Google Privacy Policy.

Retention and deletion

We keep account data while your account is active. You may request deletion by emailing us.

Contact

Privacy questions: fazone2026@gmail.com
